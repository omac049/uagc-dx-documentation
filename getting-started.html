Understanding the DX & Enrollment Funnel

The Digital Experience (DX) team plays a critical role in the student enrollment funnel, optimizing every touchpoint from first visit to enrollment completion.

2. Interest‚Äã

  • Lead capture forms

3. Consideration‚Äã
